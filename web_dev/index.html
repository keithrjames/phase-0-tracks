Categories of B-boying (a.k.a Break-dancing)

B-boying is well known for its incredible feats of strength and balance, but that is not the only piece of this varied style of dance. There are 3 basic components to B-boying:

  1. Uprock: The movement the dancer preforms while standing, most comparable to conventional dances
  2. Downrock: The movement a bboy preforms on the ground, usually on the hands in feet in circular motion of some variation
  3. Power Moves/Freezes: The moves that come to mind when you imagine breakdancing, these are moves and poses routed in being difficult to pull off and are incredibly flashy
These are just the basic elements of course, and there are many more elements such as:
  • transitions
  • moves borrowed from other dance styles
